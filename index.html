DEPARTMENT: MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Thiagarajar College of Engineering (TCE) is a prestigious government-aided institution located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1957 by philanthropist and industrialist Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar, TCE has been a beacon of technical education in the region. Affiliated with Anna University and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the college offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology.
